Our Story

Made with
purpose.
Built with
pride.

Pika Furniture was born out of a frustration with furniture that looked good in a showroom but fell apart within a few years. We set out to make things differently — with better materials, proper craft, and no compromises on quality.

Today, every piece we make is still manufactured in-house at our workshop, by a team of craftspeople who take enormous personal pride in the work that leaves under the Pika name.

Pika Furniture was founded in 2012 by two friends who had grown tired of the same story: beautiful design, disposable quality. Working from a small rented workshop with a handful of tools and a lot of determination, they began making tables and chairs for friends and family.

Word spread. Within two years, they had outgrown the workshop twice. By 2016, Pika had its own dedicated manufacturing facility and a small but fiercely committed team. The philosophy remained unchanged: source the best materials, build things properly, and stand behind every piece unconditionally.

Today, Pika Furniture offers over 50 designs across six categories — from living room to outdoor — while still manufacturing every item in-house. We've delivered more than 3,000 pieces to homes, offices, restaurants, and hotels across the country.

What We Believe

Our Four Principles

01
Craftsmanship

Every joint, surface, and finish is handled by skilled hands. We don't outsource, cut corners, or rush. The work shows.

02
Sustainability

We use FSC-certified timber, water-based finishes, and reclaimed materials wherever possible. Waste is not acceptable.

03
Longevity

We design for decades, not seasons. Our pieces are repairable, refinishable, and built to outlast the trends that surrounded them.

04
Transparency

We'll tell you where your materials come from, how long it will take, and what it costs — honestly and without spin.
